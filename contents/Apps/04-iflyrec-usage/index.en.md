# iFLYREC: Fast Speech-to-Text for Meetings and Subtitles

**Why use it?** Turn multilingual speech into **accurate, exportable** text without wiring up scripts or raw speech APIs. The UI keeps language, speakers, and domain settings visible—great for meeting notes, reviews, and subtitle drafts.

Compared with “roll your own” speech stacks, you spend **less time debugging** and **more time with a clean transcript**.

## Home Screen at a Glance

After login, the home screen highlights:

| Feature | Best for |
|---------|----------|
| **Start Recording** | Live capture with light polishing—quick field notes |
| **Import File** | Audio/video to text, multilingual—reviews and subtitles |
| **Floating Captions** | Floating live captions while you use other apps |

**Recent Files** lists past jobs; files live in the cloud so phone and desktop stay in sync and local loss is less likely.

Below we focus on **Import File**. Tap **Import File** to open file transcription.

![](images/Pasted%20image%2020260328002717.png)

## Upload and settings (where quality is won)

Pick the **source language** carefully—defaults include English-only, Chinese–English mix, and more. The right language choice **usually improves** recognition a lot.

![](images/Pasted%20image%2020260328011832.png)

Tap **More** for additional languages (e.g., Spanish, Japanese, Chinese) and enhanced modes such as **English Pro** or **Chinese–English mix Pro**.

![](images/Pasted%20image%2020260328012651.png)

Drag your audio or video into **Select or drag**.

![](images/Pasted%20image%2020260328012830.png)

**Speaker**: Set the number of speakers; choose **Auto** if unsure. Our sample is single-speaker, so we use **1**. More accurate metadata tends to mean cleaner output.

![](images/Pasted%20image%2020260328013110.png)

**Professional Domains**: Match the content (law, economics, medicine, etc.) to help terminology and phrasing.

![](images/Pasted%20image%2020260328013338.png)

If nothing fits, use **Keyword Optimization** to supply terms—the built-in AI uses them to tune recognition and wording.

![](images/Pasted%20image%2020260328013619.png)

Tap **Submit** to upload; you’ll see progress.

![](images/Pasted%20image%2020260328013814.png)

![](images/Pasted%20image%2020260328013901.png)

When **Uploading** switches to **Open File**, tap it to start transcription.

![](images/Pasted%20image%2020260328014036.png)

## Tasks and preview

During or after processing, the sidebar shows the job; use the mic icon to jump there quickly.

![](images/Pasted%20image%2020260328014238.png)

Click a sentence and the waveform seeks to that moment—handy for **spot-checking** accuracy.

![](images/Pasted%20image%2020260328014511.png)

The settings near the waveform control preview behavior:

- **Display speaker**: Show speaker names above lines, chat-style  
- **Speaker filtering**: Play only selected voices—noise reduction or focus on key speakers  
- **Display timecode**: Show start times above segments  
- **Skip silent segments**: Hide silent gaps for a tighter read  

![](images/Pasted%20image%2020260328020116.png)

The translate control translates the full document.

>[!TIP]
>
> iFLYREC can translate many source languages **into Chinese**. For other target languages, export first, then use another tool.

![](images/Pasted%20image%2020260328014750.png)

## Export

Open **Downloads** to export.

![](images/Pasted%20image%2020260328014640.png)

Choose **docx**, **srt**, **txt**, etc., and whether to include speaker labels, timecodes, and other metadata.

![](images/Pasted%20image%2020260328015351.png)

Tap **Download** to save locally.

![](images/Pasted%20image%2020260328015733.png)

![](images/Pasted%20image%2020260328015824.png)

You now have a usable transcript. For video subtitles, **srt** is usually the right pick.

![](images/Pasted%20image%2020260328015906.png)

**Bottom line:** iFLYREC shines when you need **fast, high-volume** meeting or lecture text. The app also bundles other AI helpers—explore as needed beyond this walkthrough.
